=============================================================================
4. BIGGEST RISKS TO SIGNAL VALIDITY
=============================================================================

1. REGIME SENSITIVITY (primary risk)
   One bearish month completely inverts the signal (t=-3.27 in October).
   Without a reliable real-time regime indicator (SPY/QQQ not yet in cache),
   the signal cannot be safely traded in its current form. A regime filter
   is required but cannot be validated on 1 bearish month.

2. PRICE TIER SPECIFICITY (secondary risk — also an opportunity)
   Signal only works for $10-20 stocks. This narrows the universe significantly
   and must be explicitly encoded in the condition definition for phase_r3.

3. LIMITED DATA WINDOW (structural constraint)
   Only 6 months of data. Only 1 bearish month. Cannot validate cross-year
   stability or characterize regime transitions. This is an API limitation.
   Workaround: rebuild cache with 2024-03-25 to 2025-06-30 window to add
   ~15 more months of data. This would provide at minimum 1-2 more bearish
   periods for regime filter validation.

4. SPECULATIVE NAME CONCENTRATION
   Even in non-bearish months, top 10 = 53.7% of return. The best-performing
   tickers are crypto miners (MARA, RIOT, CLSK), EV plays (RIVN, LCID), and
   other speculative names. These have limited capacity for actual allocation.

5. CONCLUSION AND FORWARD PATH
=============================================================================

Phase_r2 verdict: PARENT_002 NARROWED — does not advance as currently defined.

The signal is not structurally broad across all price tiers and all regimes.
The t=2.33 from phase_r1 is real but conceals two layers of instability:
  (a) regime sensitivity — collapses in bearish markets
  (b) price-tier specificity — only $10-20 range stocks

RECOMMENDED FORWARD PATH:

Spawn child_001 from parent_002 with the following condition:
  1. drive_down with abs magnitude >= 2.0%
  2. Stock session open in $5-20 range (tightened price filter)
  3. Price reclaims session_open post-drive
  4. Market regime filter: non-bearish (to be defined using SPY or universe-avg)

Prerequisite before child_001 can advance to phase_r3:
  (a) Build SPY daily cache (or extend intraday cache to 2024-03-25 to cover more regimes)
  (b) Run the extended window analysis to confirm regime sensitivity is not a single-month fluke
  (c) Verify at least 2-3 bearish months exist in the expanded dataset

Without (a)+(b), the regime filter cannot be validated and child_001 cannot
safely be formalized into a phase_r3 strategy.
